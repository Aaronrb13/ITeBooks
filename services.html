SSARC has the capacity to conduct Computer Assisted Telephone Interviewing (CATI), as well as provide a platform for web-based surveys. All aspects of the project are developed in collaboration with the SSARC faculty and the client. These may include: Instrument design Survey dissemination Data collection Management auditing Data analytics Multimedia reports
